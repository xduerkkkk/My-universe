
####  **针对“引言/谚语类” (The Quote)**

- **逻辑：** 引出名言 -> 解释含义 -> 也就是我的观点。
    
- **模板：**
    
    > A widespread proverb / The statement "**[抄题目里的名言]**" has recently sparked a heated discussion. Simple as it sounds, it conveys a profound truth: **[用简单句解释一下意思]**. To my understanding, it serves as a wake-up call that **[你的核心观点: e.g., innovation is the key to success]**.
    

#### **2. 针对“图画/图表类” (The Picture)**

- **逻辑：** 描述图片 -> 揭示寓意。
    
- **模板：**
    
    > As is vividly depicted in the cartoon, **[描述图片内容: e.g., a man is struggling to...]**. The caption reads: "**[图片上的字]**". This picture intends to inform us that **[你的核心观点]**.
