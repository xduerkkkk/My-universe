### **实战套用演示**

**题目：** Specialization vs. Versatility in Career Development

**第一段：引入**  
With the rapid development of society, the debate over **whether to focus on specialization or versatility** has become a recurrent focus of public concern. It is therefore imperative that we take it seriously. **To cope with the changing job market, priority should be given to versatility.**

**第二段：论证 (Why + How)**  
**Firstly, [acquiring a broad range of skills] is of paramount importance.**  
Take myself as an example. If I had failed to **[learn extra skills like computer programming]**, I would never have **[found a good internship]**. This proves that **[versatility]** acts as a decisive factor.  
**Many people tend to confine themselves to [a single narrow field], whereas reality demands [comprehensive talents].** (你看，这里用这就非常完美！)  
Therefore, it is vital for us to seize every opportunity to **[expand our knowledge boundaries]**.

**Furthermore, another key aspect lies in [cultivating cross-disciplinary abilities].** (跨学科能力)  
Instead of **relying passively on [limited expertise]**, we should **proactively [embrace new challenges]**. In this competitive era, failing to **[become versatile]** may result in **unwanted consequences**, such as **being eliminated by the industry**.

**第三段：总结**  
**In conclusion, based on the discussions above, I firmly believe that [developing versatility] requires our utmost attention.**  
**It is high time that we took immediate action.**  
Only by **becoming all-round talents**, can we ensure that the future of our careers will be **promising and brilliant**.
