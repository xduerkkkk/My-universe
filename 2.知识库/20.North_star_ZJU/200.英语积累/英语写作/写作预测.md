

# 1**题目：** Specialization vs. Versatility in Career Development

**第一段：引入**  
With the rapid development of society, the debate over **whether to focus on specialization or versatility** has become a recurrent focus of public concern. It is therefore imperative that we take it seriously. **To cope with the changing job market, priority should be given to versatility.**

**第二段：论证 (Why + How)**  
**Firstly, [acquiring a broad range of skills] is of paramount importance.**  
Take myself as an example. If I had failed to **[learn extra skills like computer programming]**, I would never have **[found a good internship]**. This proves that **[versatility]** acts as a decisive factor.  
**Many people tend to confine themselves to [a single narrow field], whereas reality demands [comprehensive talents].** (你看，这里用这就非常完美！)  
Therefore, it is vital for us to seize every opportunity to **[expand our knowledge boundaries]**.

**Furthermore, another key aspect lies in [cultivating cross-disciplinary abilities].** (跨学科能力)  
Instead of **relying passively on [limited expertise]**, we should **proactively [embrace new challenges]**. In this competitive era, failing to **[become versatile]** may result in **unwanted consequences**, such as **being eliminated by the industry**.

**第三段：总结**  
**In conclusion, based on the discussions above, I firmly believe that [developing versatility] requires our utmost attention.**  
**It is high time that we took immediate action.**  
Only by **becoming all-round talents**, can we ensure that the future of our careers will be **promising and brilliant**.


# **题目：** Addressing Food Waste in College Cafeterias

**第一段：引入 (现象描述)**  
With the rapid development of society, the phenomenon of **food waste in college cafeterias** has become a recurrent focus of public concern. Despite widespread efforts, it remains a noticeable problem. It is therefore imperative that we take it seriously. **To cope with this trend, priority should be given to the following aspects.**

**第二段：论证 (Why + How)**  
**Firstly, [cultivating the virtue of frugality] is of paramount importance.** (把不浪费上升为“美德”)  
**Take myself as an example.** If I had failed to **[finish my meals every day]**, I would never have **[realized how hard it is to produce grain]**. This proves that **[frugality]** acts as a decisive factor.  
**Many people tend to confine themselves to [wasteful habits], whereas reality demands [sustainable lifestyle].** (完美适配！浪费 vs 可持续)  
Therefore, it is vital for us to seize every opportunity to **[order food rationally according to our needs]**. (具体的行动A：按需点餐)

**Furthermore, another key aspect lies in [strengthening supervision and management].** (除了个人，还得靠管理)  
Instead of **relying passively on [students' self-discipline]**, colleges should **proactively [launch the "Clear Your Plate" campaign]**. (光盘行动)  
In this resource-constrained era, failing to **[curb food waste]** may result in **unwanted consequences**, such as **environmental pollution and economic loss**. (扣题：原文提到的环境和经济担忧)

**第三段：总结**  
**In conclusion, based on the discussions above, I firmly believe that [addressing food waste] requires our utmost attention.**  
**It is high time that we took immediate action.**  
Only by **doing so**, can we ensure that the future of our society will be **promising and brilliant**.

# **题目：** "Attitude is a little thing that makes a big difference"

**第一段：引入 (引言模板)**  
The statement "**Attitude is a little thing that makes a big difference**" has recently sparked a heated discussion. Simple as it sounds, it conveys a profound truth: **our mental outlook determines how far we can go in life**. To my understanding, it serves as a wake-up call that **[a positive attitude]** is the key to success.

**第二段：论证 (Why + How)**  
**Firstly, [maintaining optimism] is of paramount importance.** (把态度具体化为“保持乐观”)  
**Take myself as an example.** If I had failed to **[keep a positive mind when facing difficulties]**, I would never have **[overcome the setbacks in my study]**. This proves that **[attitude]** acts as a decisive factor.  
**Many people tend to confine themselves to [pessimism and complaints], whereas reality demands [confidence and courage].** (对比：悲观抱怨 vs 自信勇气)  
Therefore, it is vital for us to seize every opportunity to **[adjust our mindset]**. (行动A：调整心态)

**Furthermore, another key aspect lies in [facing challenges with a smile].** (态度的一种具体表现)  
Instead of **relying passively on [luck or giving up easily]**, we should **proactively [confront obstacles]**.  
In this competitive era, failing to **[have a right attitude]** may result in **unwanted consequences**, such as **losing precious opportunities**.

**第三段：总结**  
**In conclusion, based on the discussions above, I firmly believe that [cultivating a good attitude] requires our utmost attention.**  
**It is high time that we took immediate action.**  
Only by **doing so**, can we ensure that the future of our lives will be **promising and brilliant**.
